<h3>Learn and Earn Programme</h3>
<p>The Academy is an additional two-year pathway for APS graduates<strong> </strong>which
offers a <strong>Learn and Earn Programme </strong>to deepen their work
skills.</p>
<p>Under the programme, the Academy students continue to <strong>Learn </strong>workforce
skills (WSQ) and they get to <strong>Earn </strong>allowance for their On-the-Job
Training (OJT).</p>
