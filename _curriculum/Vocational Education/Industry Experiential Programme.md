<h1>Industry Experiential Programme (IEP)</h1>
<p>An important element of the vocational training is the structured industrial
attachment, IEP. Students apply vocational skills learnt in a real-world
context during IEP. This is also a good exposure for students to understand
more about the industry real work.</p>
